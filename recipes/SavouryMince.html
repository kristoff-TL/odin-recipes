Savoury Mince





Description

Savoury mince is a dish that pleases most taste palettes due to it's simplicity. With versatile ingredients, your savoury mince can be used to accompany other meals or be a meal on it's own. Depending on your audience, add some curry powder to give it a kick or maybe some honey if you like some sweetness. I personally enjoy mixing up the flavours time to time.



Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 garlic clove, chopped finely
  • 500 gram beef mince, lean
  • 2 carrots, sliced and then diced
  • 1 cup fresh green beans, top and tail
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • 2 cups finely chopped red cabbage
  • 1 tbsp mild curry powder
  • 1 tspn tumeric powder
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tbsp crushed black pepper
  • 1 pinch salt


Method

  1. Heat the oil in a large frying pan on medium. Saute onion and garlic for 2-3 minutes until onion is tender.
  2. Add mince either defrosted or frozen. Cook until mince is well browned and broken up
    1. If defrosted, mince will fall apart easily.
    2. If frozen, let one side of the mince sear, flip it over, use a fork to scrape the mince into the pan, repeat until all mince is broken apart.
  3. Stir in carrot, frozen peas and green beans. Add curry powder, tumeric, and cup of water then leave to simmer on a low heat for 35 minutes.
  4. Once carrot begins to soften add honey and stir in
  5. When carrot is soft take of heat and leave to rest for 5 minutes
